About

This website was created by Alistair Gibb. It uses the Web Audio API and Tone.js as the audio framework.

Weather data is is taken from the UK Met Office Data Point, this is public sector information licensed under the Open Government Licence. Thank you to them that such data has been made available to the public through Data Point. For clarity, I must confirm that this project is not affliated or endorsed by the UK Met Office.
